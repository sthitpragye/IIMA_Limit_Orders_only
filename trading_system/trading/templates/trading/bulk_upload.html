CSV Format Guidelines

The first row must be the exact header row. Columns must be in this order:

  • Roll — unique, numbers only
  • Name — alphabets only
  • Mail — valid email (must contain @ and .)
  • Role — exactly TRADER or MARKET_MAKER
  • Password — mix of alphabets, numbers and special characters
Roll,Name,Mail,Role,Password
10234,AliceSmith,alice@iitk.ac.in,TRADER,Alice@123
10235,BobJones,bob@iitk.ac.in,MARKET_MAKER,Bob#456
10236,CarolWu,carol@iitk.ac.in,TRADER,Carol$789
